5. Testosterone and Hormone Estimates

TRT Lab uses two-compartment pharmacokinetic modeling based on published pharmacokinetic data and FDA prescribing information to estimate blood serum testosterone and estradiol levels over time. These estimates are mathematical projections only and are subject to significant individual variation.

You acknowledge and agree that:

  • Estimated levels are not laboratory measurements and should not be treated as such
  • Actual serum concentrations depend on factors including individual metabolism, injection technique, body composition, SHBG levels, concurrent medications, and adherence — many of which the model cannot fully account for
  • Peak, trough, and steady-state estimates are approximations intended to illustrate general trends, not precise clinical values
  • You should rely on actual laboratory blood tests performed by accredited laboratories and interpreted by qualified healthcare providers for clinical decision-making
