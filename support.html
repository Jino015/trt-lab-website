Support

We're here to help

Have a question about TRT Lab? Whether you need help with a technical issue, have a billing question about your premium subscription, or want to suggest a new feature — reach out and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Contact Support

trtlabsupport@gmail.com

Bug Reports

Found something that isn't working correctly? Let us know what happened, which device and operating system you're using, and the steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots are always helpful.

Subscriptions

Questions about TRT Pro billing, restoring purchases, or managing your subscription? We can help guide you through the process. For refunds, you may also contact Apple Support or Google Play Support directly.

Feature Requests

Have an idea that would make TRT Lab better? We'd love to hear it. Share what you'd like to see and how it would help your protocol tracking workflow.

Account Questions

TRT Lab does not require an account — your data is stored locally on your device. If you have questions about data storage, privacy, transferring to a new device, or recovering premium access, we're happy to assist.
